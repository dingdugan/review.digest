### 🚨 Negative themes

**Unjustified bans, strikes & age restrictions** (~30, US/GB/DE) — Automated moderation banning/age-restricting/muting users who claim no wrongdoing; opaque, bot-driven appeals that repeat the same reply.
> "Automated moderation warned me for no reason (I posted the German national flag) and this led to a full account ban; my appeal was also rejected. I hadn't insulted, bullied, or broken any law." [DE]

**Moderation double standard — harmful content stays, harmless content punished** (~18, US/GB/DE) — Users report nudity, gore, CP, grooming, SA jokes, racism, and threats going unactioned after reporting, while jokes/mild comments get strikes.
> "I got groomed and when I reported it, no action was taken." [GB]

**Inappropriate/nude TikTok Shop ads & ad overload** (~10, US/GB/DE) — Lingerie/nude Shop ads recur despite "not interested"; ads every 2–4 videos, some pornographic.

**Comments hidden/removed & replies not appearing** (~9, US/GB) — Comments silently filtered or deleted with no flag/appeal, replies invisible to others, sticker-comments auto-deleted.
> "My replies won't show up in the comment section... it's pathetic they still haven't added an edit option for comments." [US]

**Algorithm suppression & views throttling** (~8, US/GB/DE) — Creators report sudden view collapse (100k→300), suspected shadowbans, and paid promotion delivering fake engagement.
